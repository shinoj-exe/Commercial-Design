About us

Every Day, 28 people dependent on agriculture commit suicide in India. Surprisingly, landed farmers account for 86 per cent of the suicides.

That's my motivation to begin Kanchan Overseas. I am Harpit Agrawal, Proprietor, Kanchan Overseas. After completing a master's in Business administration, and serveing for 2 years in corporates worId I felt a need for change in Indian Agricuture. Thus embarked on journey based on my philosopy.

My philosophy is "Agriculture is Business".

Farmers need to think and act like businessmen. They need to adopt practices which adds value in terms of safety of their produce for human consumption; increase productivity; and maintains sustainaibility of their farms aganist climate change.

Our Mission

Bring inspiration and systems of change in Indian Agriculture.

Indian agriculture is far from resolving issues of pesticide residue levels and low production levels per hectare. Meanwhile exposure to pesticides are causing diseases and low production levels are concern to world's population which are projected to reach 9.8 Billion by 2050.

Outcomes we intend to achieve through our mission

Prosperity: inspire farmers to accept good agriculture practices to make agriculture rewarding financially.

Reliability: to become reliable supplier of agriculture produce who meets customer requirments

Sustainaibility: regenerate farms not only to withstand but have positive impact on global climate change.
