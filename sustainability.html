Sustainability

‘Sustainability is the core value at Kanchan Overseas, and one of the aspects on which we are founded’.

At Kanchan Overseas, we are committed to being a responsible and sustainable business that generates value for all the stakeholders it interacts with. We take into account the farmers, suppliers, customers and even the need of our end users further downstream. While by the very nature of our business, our environmental footprint is minimal, we believe it is our responsibility as global citizens to address the challenge posed by diminishing natural resources and various aspects of climate change to transform them into new opportunities for responsible economic growth.

Sustainaible Procurement

Our products are agricultural produce. Through our sustainable procurement practices, we seek to forge strong and long-lasting relationships with farmers and suppliers to strengthen agricultural productivity and increase yield sustainably. This will also ensure we offer consistent quality products to our customers.

We are committed to the highest standards of ethical conduct and social and environmental responsibility. We expect our suppliers to aspire to these standards in their business operations.

Ethical Business Practices

Our code of ethical business conduct specifies and helps with the continued implementation of the company’s values by establishing certain non-negotiable minimum standards of behaviour. It guides us on compliance, labour practices and standard, and environmental health & safety.
